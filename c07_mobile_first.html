Mobile First Design

Mobile first design is more than simply choosing a single-column layout that is touch-screeen friendly. Consider the following guiding principles:

  • relatively small screen with limited space for paragraph text
  • may have data restrictions
  • mobile browsers may not support obscure fonts or new HTML/CSS features
  • touch-only interaction means that clickable options need space to be useful
  • limited screen space means you must be more aggressive in trimming out non-essential content
  • mobile devices are often used in sunlit areas, meaning high contrast designs are more important
